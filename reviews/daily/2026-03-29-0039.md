## Step-27 rationale
`xibi/condensation.py` is the architectural prerequisite for the observation cycle (Step 8).
Currently channel content (email body) reaches LLM roles raw — full boilerplate, legal
footers, quote blocks, raw URLs. The condensation pipeline:
- Strips noise (boilerplate, footers, quoted lines, signatures)
- Replaces URLs with [link] (phishing defense)
- Counts links and attachments
- Detects phishing signals (urgency + wire transfer, CEO impersonation, display/domain mismatch)
- Truncates to 2000-char cap
- Assigns stable ref_ids for retrievability

Additionally modifies `HeartbeatPoller._classify_email()` to pass condensed body preview to
the LLM classifier when body content is available (graceful enhancement — falls back to
From+Subject if no body).

Pure Python, no LLM calls, deterministic. 15 tests specified. One PR.
