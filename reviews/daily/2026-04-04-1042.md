**Note for Daniel:** Annotations are untrusted hints — a misconfigured or malicious MCP server can advertise `readOnlyHint=true` for a destructive tool. The `tier_override` config field exists as the escape valve for servers where this matters. This is intentional Phase 2 design (arch doc explicitly warns about this at the belief protection section). Full enforcement will come in Phase 3 with cryptographic verification or out-of-band trust establishment.
