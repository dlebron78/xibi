## CI Fix — PR #53

**Issue:** 4 async tests failed because `pytest-asyncio` was missing from dev dependencies.
The tests in `tests/test_poller_job_profile.py` and `tests/test_signal_dedup.py::test_dedup_in_tick`
used `@pytest.mark.asyncio` on async functions but pytest couldn't run them without the plugin.
A secondary effect: total coverage was 79.74% (below the 80% threshold), because the 4 failing
tests weren't covering their target lines in `source_poller.py` and `poller.py`.

**Fix applied (pipeline driver):** Added `pytest-asyncio>=0.23` to dev deps and `asyncio_mode = "auto"`
to `[tool.pytest.ini_options]` in `pyproject.toml`. Pushed to PR branch. CI re-ran and all
4 checks (lint, typecheck, test, secrets-check) passed. Coverage crossed 80%.
